Eligibilty

The process to apply for a CardHut No Cost EMI Card online is quick and simple.
You can apply for it if you meet the following eligibilty criteria:

EMI Card

You should be between 21 and 60 years of age to apply for an CardHut No Cost EMI Card.

Insta EMI Card

You should be between 25 and 60 years of age to apply for an CardHut No Cost EMI Card.

Income Source

You must have a regular source of income.




FAQ Section
